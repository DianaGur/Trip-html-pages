Krabi, Thailand

Krabi is a province in southern Thailand known for its stunning beaches, limestone cliffs, and vibrant night market.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A picturesque beach accessible only by boat, surrounded by towering cliffs and crystal-clear waters.. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • Tiger Cave Temple – A sacred Buddhist temple famous for its stunning architecture and panoramic views of Krabi Town.. Hours: 8:00–17:00
  • Krabi Town Night Market – A bustling night market offering a variety of street food, souvenirs, and local goods.. Hours: 18:00–22:00
