Santorini, Greece

Santorini is a picturesque Greek island known for its whitewashed houses, blue-domed churches, and breathtaking sunsets.

Recommended Attractions

  • Oia Village – This charming village is famous for its stunning sunsets and offers breathtaking views of the caldera.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Akrotiri archaeological site – Explore the ancient city of Akrotiri, which was destroyed by the volcanic eruption that formed the caldera.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Santorini Cable Car – Take a ride on the cable car for panoramic views of the caldera and the sea.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
