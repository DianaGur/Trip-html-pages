Krabi, Thailand

Krabi is a southern province of Thailand known for its stunning beaches, lush islands, and dramatic limestone cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A picturesque beach surrounded by towering cliffs and only accessible by boat, perfect for swimming and relaxing.. Hours: 09:00–17:00
  • James Bond Island – A small island famous for its appearance in the James Bond movie 'The Man with the Golden Gun', featuring a distinctive shape and turquoise waters.. Hours: 09:00–17:00
  • Tonsai Beach – A secluded beach with crystal-clear waters, surrounded by towering cliffs and accessible only by boat or via a challenging hike.. Hours: 09:00–17:00
