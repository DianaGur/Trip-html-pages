Koh Tao, Thailand

Koh Tao is a tropical island in the Gulf of Thailand, known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and vibrant marine life.

Recommended Attractions

  • Sai Daeng Beach – A picturesque beach with a scenic viewpoint and snorkeling opportunities.. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • Chalok Baan Khao Viewpoint – A stunning viewpoint with panoramic views of the island and the surrounding waters.. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • Mae Haad Beach – A popular beach with clear waters, powdery sand, and a lively atmosphere.. Hours: 9:00–18:00
