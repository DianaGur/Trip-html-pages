Bali, Indonesia

A tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, temples, and vibrant culture.

Recommended Attractions

  • Tirta Empul Temple – A sacred water temple where visitors can purify themselves in holy springs.. Hours: 8:00–17:00
  • Uluwatu Temple – A Balinese Hindu temple perched on a cliff overlooking the Indian Ocean.. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • Ubud Monkey Forest – A nature reserve and temple complex home to over 700 Balinese long-tailed macaques.. Hours: 8:00–18:00
