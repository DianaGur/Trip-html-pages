Phuket, Thailand

Phuket is a tropical island in southern Thailand known for its stunning beaches, clear waters, and vibrant nightlife.

Recommended Attractions

  • Patong Beach – One of Phuket's most popular beaches, Patong is known for its white sandy shores, lively nightlife, and wide range of dining options.. Hours: 9:00–22:00
  • Big Buddha Temple – A 45-meter-tall statue of the Buddha, this temple is a must-visit for its stunning architecture and peaceful atmosphere.. Hours: 8:00–18:00
  • Phang Nga Bay – A must-visit for boat tours, Phang Nga Bay is known for its towering limestone karsts and emerald waters.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
