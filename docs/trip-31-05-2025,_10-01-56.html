Tulum, Mexico

Tulum is a coastal town on the Caribbean coast of Mexico known for its stunning beaches, ancient Mayan ruins, and vibrant coral reefs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Tulum National Park – Explore the ancient Mayan ruins of Tulum, perched on a cliff overlooking the Caribbean Sea.. Hours: 8:00–17:00
  • Sian Ka'an Biosphere Reserve – Take a boat tour through the mangrove forests and coral reefs of this protected area, home to dolphins, manatees, and over 300 bird species.. Hours: 8:00–17:00
  • Xel-Há Park – Swim in the crystal-clear waters of this natural aquarium, featuring an underground river, waterfalls, and a variety of marine life.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
