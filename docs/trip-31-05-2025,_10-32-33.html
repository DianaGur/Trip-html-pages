Ha Long Bay, Vietnam

A stunning natural wonder with over 1,600 limestone islands and islets rising out of emerald green waters.

Recommended Attractions

  • Ha Long Bay Cruise – Take a day or overnight cruise to explore the bay's hidden caves, grottos, and secluded beaches.. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • Bo Hon Island – A popular island with a stunning cave system and great snorkeling opportunities.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Thien Cung Cave – A breathtaking cave with stunning stalactites and stalagmites, featuring a beautiful lake and stunning views.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
