Krabi, Thailand

Krabi is a southern Thai province known for its stunning beaches, lush jungles, and dramatic limestone cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A picturesque beach surrounded by towering cliffs and accessible only by boat. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • Tonsai Beach – A secluded beach with crystal-clear waters and a relaxed atmosphere. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • Krabi Town Night Market – A bustling night market offering a wide range of local street food, souvenirs, and shopping. Hours: 18:00–22:00
