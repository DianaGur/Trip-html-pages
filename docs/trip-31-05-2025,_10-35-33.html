Santorini, Greece

A picturesque Greek island in the Aegean Sea, known for its whitewashed houses, blue-domed churches, and stunning sunsets.

Recommended Attractions

  • Oia Village – A charming village perched on the caldera rim, offering breathtaking views of the sea and surrounding landscape.. Hours: 9:00–20:00
  • Akrotiri Archaeological Site – A well-preserved ancient city frozen in time by the volcanic eruption of 1625 BC, featuring impressive frescoes and architecture.. Hours: 8:00–19:00
  • Imerovigli Village – A scenic village with stunning views of the caldera, featuring a castle, a museum, and several restaurants and shops.. Hours: 9:00–20:00
