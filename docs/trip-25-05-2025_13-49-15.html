Krabi, Thailand

Krabi is a southern province of Thailand known for its stunning beaches, islands, and dramatic limestone cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A secluded beach accessible only by boat, surrounded by towering cliffs and crystal-clear waters.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Ao Nang Beach – A popular beach with a long stretch of sand, lined with restaurants, shops, and bars.. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • James Bond Island – A small island famous for its appearance in the James Bond movie 'The Man with the Golden Gun'.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
