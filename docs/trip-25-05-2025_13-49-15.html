Krabi, Thailand

Krabi is a southern province of Thailand known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and dramatic limestone cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A secluded beach accessible only by boat, known for its tall cliffs, white sand, and crystal-clear waters.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Phang Nga Bay – A bay famous for its towering limestone karsts and turquoise waters, popular for boat tours and snorkeling.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Krabi Town Night Market – A bustling night market offering a variety of street food, souvenirs, and local handicrafts.. Hours: 17:00–22:00
