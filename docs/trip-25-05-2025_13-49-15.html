Krabi, Thailand

A coastal province in southern Thailand known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and dramatic limestone cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A secluded beach only accessible by boat, surrounded by towering cliffs and offering crystal-clear waters and powdery sand.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Tiger Cave Temple – A sacred Buddhist temple featuring a giant golden Buddha and offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape.. Hours: 8:00–18:00
  • Khao Ngon National Park – A scenic national park featuring hiking trails, waterfalls, and caves, offering a glimpse into Krabi's lush natural beauty.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
