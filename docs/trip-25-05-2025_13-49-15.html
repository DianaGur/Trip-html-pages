Krabi, Thailand

A province in southern Thailand known for its stunning beaches, islands, and dramatic limestone cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Tiger Cave Temple – A Buddhist temple complex located in the heart of Krabi Town, featuring stunning architecture and a massive golden chedi.. Hours: 8:00–17:00
  • Railay Beach – A secluded beach accessible only by boat, surrounded by towering cliffs and lush vegetation.. Hours: 24/7
  • Krabi Town Night Market – A bustling night market filled with street food, souvenirs, and local handicrafts.. Hours: 18:00–22:00
