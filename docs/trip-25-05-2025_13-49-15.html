Krabi, Thailand

Krabi is a southern province in Thailand known for its stunning beaches, limestone cliffs, and vibrant night markets.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A secluded beach accessible only by boat, surrounded by towering cliffs and crystal-clear waters.. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • Tiger Cave Temple – A popular Buddhist temple featuring a stunning view of Krabi Town and the surrounding area.. Hours: 8:00–17:00
  • Ao Nang Beach – A lively beach with a long stretch of white sand and a variety of restaurants, bars, and shops.. Hours: 6:00–22:00
