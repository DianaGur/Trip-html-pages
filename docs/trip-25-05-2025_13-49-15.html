Krabi, Thailand

Krabi is a southern Thai province known for its stunning beaches, lush islands, and dramatic limestone cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A picturesque beach surrounded by towering cliffs and accessible only by boat.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Tonsai Beach – A popular beach for rock climbing and swimming, surrounded by dramatic limestone cliffs.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Krabi Town – The provincial capital, offering a mix of modern and traditional Thai culture.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
