Krabi, Thailand

Krabi is a southern Thai province known for its stunning beaches, towering cliffs, and lush rainforests.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A pristine beach surrounded by towering cliffs, only accessible by boat. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Krabi Town Night Market – A bustling night market offering street food, souvenirs, and local crafts. Hours: 18:00–22:00
  • Tiger Cave Temple – A sacred Buddhist temple with stunning views of Krabi Town. Hours: 8:00–16:00
