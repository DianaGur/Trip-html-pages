Krabi, Thailand

A tropical paradise on the southern coast of Thailand, known for its stunning beaches, islands, and limestone cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A secluded beach surrounded by towering cliffs and accessible only by boat, perfect for swimming and rock climbing.. Hours: 09:00–17:00
  • Tonsai Beach – A picturesque beach with crystal-clear waters and a dramatic backdrop of cliffs, popular among rock climbers and beachgoers.. Hours: 09:00–17:00
  • Krabi Town Night Market – A bustling night market offering a wide range of local street food, souvenirs, and handicrafts.. Hours: 18:00–22:00
