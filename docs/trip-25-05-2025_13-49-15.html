Krabi, Thailand

Krabi is a southern province of Thailand known for its stunning beaches, lush forests, and towering cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A secluded beach only accessible by boat, surrounded by towering cliffs and lush vegetation.. Hours: 8:00–18:00
  • Krabi Town Night Market – A bustling night market offering a wide range of local street food, souvenirs, and clothing.. Hours: 18:00–22:00
  • Phang Nga Bay – A majestic bay with towering limestone cliffs and emerald waters, perfect for kayaking and boat tours.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
