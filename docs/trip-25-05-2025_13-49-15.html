Krabi, Thailand

Krabi, located on the west coast of southern Thailand, is a popular tourist destination known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush islands. Visit Krabi for an unforgettable experience of relaxation, adventure, and cultural exploration.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach

    Accessed only by boat, Railay Beach is a secluded paradise with towering cliffs, white sandy beach, and turquoise waters.

    Opening Hours: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

  • Khao Kanab Nam

    A pair of towering cliffs that stand 100 meters above the sea, offering breathtaking views of the Krabi River and surrounding landscape.

    Opening Hours: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

  • Emerald Pool

    A series of crystal-clear pools and waterfalls surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for swimming and relaxation.

    Opening Hours: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
