Krabi, Thailand

A southern province of Thailand known for its stunning beaches, islands, and limestone cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A secluded beach accessible only by boat, surrounded by towering cliffs and turquoise waters.. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • Tiger Cave Temple – A 1,200-year-old Buddhist temple featuring a large golden Buddha statue and stunning views of Krabi Town.. Hours: 8:00–17:00
  • Krabi Town Night Market – A bustling night market offering a wide range of local street food, souvenirs, and souvenirs.. Hours: 18:00–22:00
