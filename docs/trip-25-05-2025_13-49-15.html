Krabi, Thailand

A tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and dramatic limestone cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A secluded beach accessible only by boat, surrounded by towering cliffs and offering breathtaking views.. Hours: 08:00–18:00
  • Tiger Cave Temple – A Buddhist temple famous for its stunning architecture, beautiful gardens, and steep climb to the top for panoramic views.. Hours: 06:00–18:00
  • Krabi Town Night Market – A bustling night market offering a wide range of street food, local souvenirs, and lively atmosphere.. Hours: 18:00–22:00
