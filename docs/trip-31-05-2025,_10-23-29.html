Santorini, Greece

A picturesque Greek island in the Cyclades group known for its whitewashed houses, blue-domed churches, and breathtaking sunsets.

Recommended Attractions

  • Oia Village – A charming village on the northern tip of Santorini with stunning views of the caldera and the sea.. Hours: 10:00–18:00
  • Akrotiri Archaeological Site – A well-preserved ancient city frozen in time by the volcanic eruption that shaped the island.. Hours: 8:00–20:00
  • Skaros Rock – A steep rock formation with stunning views of the caldera and the sea, accessible by a scenic hike.. Hours: 10:00–18:00
