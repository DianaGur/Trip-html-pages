Krabi, Thailand

A tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and dramatic limestone cliffs

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A secluded beach accessible only by boat, surrounded by towering cliffs and featuring crystal-clear waters. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Tiger Cave Temple – A sacred Buddhist temple built into the heart of a cave, featuring stunning views of Krabi Town. Hours: 8:00–17:00
  • Khao Nang Shean National Park – A lush national park featuring hiking trails, waterfalls, and stunning views of the surrounding landscape. Hours: 9:00–16:00
