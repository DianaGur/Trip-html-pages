Phuket, Thailand

A tropical paradise with white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant nightlife

Recommended Attractions

  • Patong Beach – One of Phuket's most popular beaches, known for its lively atmosphere and lively nightlife. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • Phang Nga Bay – A stunning bay with towering limestone cliffs and turquoise waters, home to James Bond Island. Hours: 8:00–17:00
  • Big Buddha Temple – A 45-meter-tall statue of Buddha, offering stunning views of the surrounding area. Hours: 8:00–17:00
