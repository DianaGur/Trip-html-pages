Paris, France

The City of Light is famous for its stunning architecture, art museums, and romantic atmosphere.

Recommended Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower – The iconic iron lattice tower is a symbol of Paris and offers breathtaking views of the city.. Hours: 9:30 AM - 11:45 PM
  • Louvre Museum – One of the world's largest and most famous museums, featuring an impressive collection of art and artifacts.. Hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Arc de Triomphe – A monumental arch honoring the soldiers who fought and died for France, offering great views of the city.. Hours: 10:00 AM - 10:30 PM
