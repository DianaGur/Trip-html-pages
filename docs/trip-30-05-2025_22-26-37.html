Krabi, Thailand

Krabi is a southern province of Thailand known for its stunning beaches, islands, and limestone cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A picturesque beach surrounded by towering cliffs, only accessible by boat. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Tiger Cave Temple – A sacred Buddhist temple with a stunning view of Krabi Town. Hours: 8:00–17:00
  • Khao Khanap Nam – A limestone hill with a beautiful waterfall and a great viewpoint. Hours: 9:00–17:00
