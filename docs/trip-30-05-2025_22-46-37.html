Krabi, Thailand

A coastal province in southern Thailand known for its stunning beaches, islands, and dramatic limestone cliffs.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A secluded beach surrounded by towering cliffs, accessible only by boat or a challenging hike.. Hours: 08:00–18:00
  • Tonsai Beach – A lively beach with rock climbing opportunities, water sports, and a relaxed atmosphere.. Hours: 06:00–20:00
  • Hong Islands – A group of islands with crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and coral reefs perfect for snorkeling and diving.. Hours: 08:00–18:00
