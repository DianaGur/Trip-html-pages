Krabi, Thailand

Krabi is a southern Thai province known for its stunning beaches, islands, and limestone cliffs

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A picturesque beach surrounded by towering cliffs, accessible only by boat. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • Tonsai Beach – A quieter beach with crystal-clear waters and a scenic view of the surrounding cliffs. Hours: 9:00–18:00
  • Krabi Town Night Market – A bustling night market offering a variety of street food, souvenirs, and local handicrafts. Hours: 18:00–22:00
