Tel Aviv, Israel

Tel Aviv is a vibrant and cosmopolitan city on Israel's Mediterranean coast, known for its stunning beaches, rich cultural heritage, and bustling nightlife.

Recommended Attractions

  • Tel Aviv Museum of Art – The museum features a diverse collection of Israeli and international art, as well as rotating exhibitions.. Hours: Sunday to Thursday: 10:00–18:00, Friday: 10:00–14:00, Saturday: 10:00–16:00
  • Jaffa Flea Market – This bustling market offers a wide range of goods, from antiques to souvenirs, and is a great place to find unique gifts or mementos.. Hours: Sunday to Friday: 8:00–17:00, Saturday: 9:00–16:00
  • Hilton Beach – One of Tel Aviv's most popular beaches, known for its lively atmosphere and stunning sunsets.. Hours: Open daily from 8:00–20:00
