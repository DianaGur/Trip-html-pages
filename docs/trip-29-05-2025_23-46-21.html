Tel Aviv, Israel

Tel Aviv is a vibrant and cosmopolitan city on Israel's Mediterranean coast, known for its stunning beaches, bustling markets, and rich cultural heritage.

Recommended Attractions

  • Tel Aviv Port – A bustling waterfront district filled with shops, restaurants, and bars, offering stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea. Hours: 10:00–22:00
  • White City of Tel Aviv – A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this beautiful collection of over 4,000 white buildings is a testament to the city's rich architectural heritage. Hours: 9:00–17:00
  • Jaffa Flea Market – A bustling marketplace filled with vintage shops, antique dealers, and street food stalls, offering a unique glimpse into Tel Aviv's past. Hours: 10:00–18:00
