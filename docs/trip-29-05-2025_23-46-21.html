Tel Aviv, Israel

Tel Aviv is a vibrant and cosmopolitan city on Israel's Mediterranean coast, known for its stunning beaches, cultural attractions, and nightlife.

Recommended Attractions

  • Tel Aviv Port – A bustling hub of shopping, dining, and entertainment, with a picturesque beach and stunning views of the city skyline.. Hours: 10:00–22:00
  • Museum of the Jewish People at Beit Hatfutsot – A comprehensive museum showcasing the history and culture of the Jewish people, with interactive exhibits and a vast collection of artifacts.. Hours: 10:00–18:00
  • Jaffa Flea Market – A lively and bustling marketplace filled with vintage shops, street food, and souvenirs, located in the historic Jaffa neighborhood.. Hours: 9:00–17:00
