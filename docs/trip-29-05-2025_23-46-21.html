Tel Aviv, Israel

A vibrant and cosmopolitan city on the Mediterranean coast, known for its stunning beaches, cultural attractions, and nightlife.

Recommended Attractions

  • Tel Aviv Museum of Art – A premier art museum featuring a diverse collection of Israeli and international art, with rotating exhibitions and installations.. Hours: 10:00–19:00
  • Jaffa Flea Market – A bustling marketplace filled with vintage shops, antique dealers, and street food vendors, offering a unique shopping and dining experience.. Hours: 10:00–17:00
  • Hilton Beach – A popular beachside spot with clear waters and soft sand, perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and people-watching.. Hours: 24/7
