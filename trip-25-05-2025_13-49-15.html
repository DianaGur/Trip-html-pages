Krabi, Thailand

A coastal province in southern Thailand known for its stunning beaches, islands, and rock climbing opportunities.

Recommended Attractions

  • Railay Beach – A secluded beach only accessible by boat, surrounded by towering cliffs and offering crystal-clear waters.. Hours: 8:00–18:00
  • Krabi Town Night Market – A bustling night market offering a wide range of local street food, souvenirs, and clothing.. Hours: 18:00–22:00
  • Tiger Cave Temple – A sacred Buddhist temple featuring a large golden pagoda and offering stunning views of the surrounding area.. Hours: 8:00–17:00
